Excutive Team Management



The Management of Jons Junior Secondary School is structured in such a way as to make certain that the ethos of the school is upheld throughout all phases of learning. This includes reviewing the strategy to align with changes within the school as well as changes to the broader education system in South Africa.

Jons Junior Secondary School Management comprises an Executive Committee and a Board of Trustees.

 

Jons Junior Secondary School School Executive Committee

The Executive Committee is responsible for overall management of the School in all its key areas of operation with a focus on policy development, planning and direction.

The Jons Junior Secondary School School Trust

Jons Junior Secondary School is “owned” by a Trust which consists mainly of parents. The Jons Junior Secondary School Trust is responsible for overall governance of the School, setting strategic direction, reviewing plans and budgets and related performance monitoring.

It is also the role of the Trust to appoint and review the Executive Head and in line with policy to delegate responsibility for operation and administration of the School to the Executive Head. It does not interfere with the day-to-day running of the School. Accordingly, the Executive Head reports to the Council on all educational and administration matters.
